About Us

Welcome to Reliance Pulses, a Trusted name since 1994. Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction sets us apart in the world of pulses and lentils.

Our Mission

Our mission at Reliance Pulses is to provide the highest quality Urad and Toor Dal, cultivated with care and precision, directly from Himmatnagar, Gujarat—the world's leading toor dal processing center. We aim to consistently deliver products that embody purity, taste, and tradition, earning the trust and loyalty of our customers both domestically and globally.

Our Plan

Reliance Pulses aims to strengthen its presence in the Indian market by enhancing distribution networks and launching campaigns that highlight the brand's heritage and quality. Globally, the company plans to expand into new markets, particularly where there is demand from the Indian diaspora. The company will continue to invest in advanced processing technology while maintaining traditional flavors.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be a global leader in the pulses industry, renowned for our commitment to quality, authenticity, and sustainability. We aspire to make Reliance Pulses a household name, symbolizing excellence in every grain and expanding our reach globally.

Services

We are committed to delivering the finest quality pulses, ensuring every grain embodies the purity and flavor you trust. Our expertise and dedication make us a leader in the pulses industry, offering a variety of services that cater to your needs.

Quality Assurance

We guarantee the highest standards of quality for our pulses, processed using advanced technology to maintain their natural taste and nutritional value.

Sustainability Initiatives

We are committed to sustainable farming practices, working closely with farmers to promote environmental responsibility and ensure the long-term health of our agricultural resources.

Custom Packaging

Our flexible packaging solutions are designed to meet the diverse needs of our customers, ensuring freshness and convenience with every purchase.

Supply Chain Excellence

Our robust supply chain management ensures timely delivery of pulses from the fields of Gujarat to your doorstep, preserving their quality throughout the journey.

Global Distribution

With a strong international presence, we export our premium pulses worldwide, bringing the authentic taste of India to families across the globe.

Customer Support

Our dedicated customer support team is always available to assist with your queries and ensure your satisfaction with our products and services.

Facts

Our dedication to quality and service has established us as a trusted name in the pulses industry.
